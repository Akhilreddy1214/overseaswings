Letter of Recommendation (LOR)

What is an LOR?

LOR is a document written by someone, typically a professor, employer, or mentor, who can provide insights into an individual's qualifications, skills, and character.LORs play a crucial role in providing a more comprehensive and qualitative evaluation of an individual beyond what grades or test scores can convey.

Purpose of LOR for Higher Education

The Purpose of an LOR for Higher Education is to offer a comprehensive evaluation of the candidate. It assesses academic achievements, personal qualities, and alignment with the requirements of a specific program or position. An LOR serves as a character reference, providing unique insights and contributing to a holistic view of the candidate beyond what traditional application materials convey. It adds credibility and trustworthiness to the candidate's application, acting as a differentiator in a competitive admissions process.

Creating a compelling Letter of Recommendation (LOR) involves focusing on key aspects:

  • Select the Right Recommender:
    • Choose someone who speaks highly of your performance or academics.
    • Ideal recommenders should be familiar with your achievements and hold a positive view of them.
    • Opt for a person in authority or a higher designation to add credibility to your application.
  • Prepare Your Achievements:
    • Before approaching the writer, gather a list of your achievements and qualities to be highlighted in the letter.
    • Seek guidance from overseas wings for assistance and content preparation.
  • Proofread Thoroughly:
    • Proofread the letter multiple times.
    • Ensure correct contact information is provided and eliminate any errors.
  • Utilize Overseas Wings LOR Services:
    • Avail overseas wings services to identify skills, qualities, or accomplishments relevant to the university program.
    • Dedicated mentors can guide you on the format, content, and assist in choosing the right recommender.
  • Overseas Wings Review Process:
    • Overseas wings reviews the letter to ensure all necessary information is included.
    • The mentor provides comprehensive information tailored to your needs, enhancing the overall quality of the letter.
