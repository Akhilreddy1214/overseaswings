Western University, located in London, Ontario, Canada, is a distinguished institution known for its commitment to academic excellence and innovation. Established in 1878, it has a long-standing tradition of producing leaders in various fields. With a diverse and vibrant campus community, Western prides itself on fostering an inclusive environment for learning and personal development. The university is renowned for its strong programs in areas such as health sciences, business, engineering, and the arts. Faculty members, often leading experts in their disciplines, are dedicated to providing a world-class education and mentorship. Western's emphasis on research and critical thinking equips students to excel in a globally competitive world.

Western University is a global community of passionate individuals focused on making change in the world today. As a top Canadian research and teaching institution, we bring together diverse minds and disciplines to power individual purpose for collective impact. The Western experience fosters meaningful connections that last a lifetime.

At Western, you will belong to a community of more than 43,000 students from 129 countries. Our unique learning environment includes exceptional academics, outstanding facilities, and internships, co-ops or practicums in every program. You can take part in diverse experiences and access the right support to develop the skills, knowledge and confidence you need to succeed.

With 400 undergraduate options and more than 150 graduate degree programs across the arts and humanities, sciences and social sciences, you can tailor your academic journey to meet your goals. Western’s 12 faculties and schools provide a full range of degree options and specializations, including professional programs in business, medicine, dentistry, engineering, law and education.

Western’s 500-hectare campus is considered one of the most beautiful in Canada, filled with trees and green space, and a mix of historic and modern buildings. Our 11 on-campus residences provide a safe and comfortable living community for students, and our home, London, Ontario is a vibrant city of 423,000 people just two hours west of Toronto.
