Required Documents for Education Loan
  • Application Form: Completed loan application form provided by the bank.
  • Proof of Admission: Admission letter from the recognized foreign university or institution.
  • Academic Records: Mark sheets and certificates of the qualifying examination (school and undergraduate level).
  • Proof of Identity: Passport, Aadhar card, PAN card, or any other government-issued photo ID.
  • Proof of Residence: Recent utility bills, rental agreement, or any other document verifying the applicant's residence.
  • Statement of Cost: Detailed breakdown of the expenses involved in the course, including tuition fees, living expenses, travel expenses, etc.
  • Collateral Documents: Documents related to collateral security (if applicable), such as property documents.
  • Income Proof:
    • Income tax returns of the co-applicant or guarantor (if any) for the last few years.
    • Salary slips or income certificate.
  • Bank Statements: Personal or family bank statements for the last 6 months.
  • Co-Applicant/Guarantor Details: Details and documents of the co-applicant or guarantor, if required.
  • Legal Documents: Any other legal documents as per the bank's requirements.
